Meat lasagna recipe

    Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles

  • cottage cheese
  • mozzerella cheese
  • misc (tomatoes, egg, onion, garlic, idk)

    Steps

  1. cook meat
  2. prep noodles
  3. light layer of sauce on bottom of baking dish
  4. layer or noodles
  5. layer sauce and cheese on top
  6. repeat steps 4-5 until out of shit
  7. bake that bad boy
  8. let cool
